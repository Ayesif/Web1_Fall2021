My Favorite Movie is Dragon Ball Super: Broly

Why is this my Favorite Movie ?!?!

Dragon Ball Super: Broly is my favorite movie because I like the Dragon Ball Series and this movie is one of the best movies made from the series.

Plot Summary

Forty-one years ago on Planet Vegeta, home of the infamous Saiyan warrior race, King Vegeta noticed a baby named Broly whose latent power exceeded that of his own son. Believing that Broly's power would one day surpass that of his child, Vegeta, the king sends Broly to the desolate planet Vampa. Broly's father Paragus follows after him, intent on rescuing his son. However, his ship gets damaged, causing the two to spend years trapped on the barren world, unaware of the salvation that would one day come from an unlikely ally. Years later on Earth, Gokuu Son and Prince Vegeta—believed to be the last survivors of the Saiyan race—are busy training on a remote island. But their sparring is interrupted when the appearance of their old enemy Frieza drives them to search for the last of the wish-granting Dragon Balls on a frozen continent. Once there, Frieza shows off his new allies: Paragus and the now extremely powerful Broly. A legendary battle that shakes the foundation of the world ensues as Gokuu and Vegeta face off against Broly, a warrior without equal whose rage is just waiting to be unleashed.

Who are the Directors and Producers ?!?!

Tatsuya Nagamine, the film director behind Dragon Ball Super: Broly. Had experience directing previous anime movies from Toei Animation. Akira Toriyama, the one behind the story, screenplay and design of characters. Also the author of Dragon Ball.Toei Animation is the animation studio that produced the entire movie. Toei Animation is the studio behind Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Super, One Piece, Sailor Moon and Digimon.

Who did the music ?!?!?!

Nohirito Sumitomo is the music behind the music of Dragon Ball Super: Broly. Sumitomo graduated from the American Berklee College of Music. He became popular when he returned to Japan and got greeted by AKAI EWI. His talent made him get compared to musical legends and got his name spread out because of this.

5 Most Important Actors

Sean Schemmel is the English Dub Voice Actor for Goku. Christopher Sabat is the English Dub Voice Actor for Vegeta. Vic Mignogna is the English Dub VA for Broly Chris Ayres is the English VA for Frieza Ian Sinclair is the English VA for Whis.

Reviews

Rotten Tomatoes gave it a 82%. "Dragon Ball Super: Broly may seem like colorful chaos to newcomers, but for longtime fans, it represents this long-running franchise near its action-packed apogee."

Would I recommend this to new viewers ?

If you love the Dragon Ball Series, you will love this movie. The movies is one hour and nine-teen minutes long. The new artstyle is really well-done and the fight scenes are really jaw-dropping. Toei Animation really knocked it put of the park with this one. The plot, the animation, the voice acting everything was really well-done. Even if you don't like Dragon Ball, you should still watch it if you like action scenes.
