Favorite Food #1:Chicken and Beef Platter

What is this food ? A chicken and beef platter is a bowl of rice, chicken and beef. It has white sauce over it and can have different sauces on top. The sides that come with it are lettuce, olives, green peppers and tomatoes.

Why I like this food.

  1. Reason 1: I used to eat it all the time in New York
  2. Reason 2: A type of food that just tastes good
  3. Reason 3: The chicken flavor blends in really nice with the rice.

Favorite Food #2: Pizza

What is this food ? A dish of Italian origin consisting of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese, typically with added meat or vegetables. It can come in another shape like a square. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

Why I like this food

  1. Reason 1: It's an old classic.
  2. Reason 2: New York Pizza got me hooked on it.
  3. Reason 3: It's filling and tastes good.
